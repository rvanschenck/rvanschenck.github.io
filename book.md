## The Reactionary Web: White Supremacist Digital Media After the Alt-Right

Digital platforms have become a critical stage for far-right movements in the US. 
This book shows how white nationalist networks exploit the Internet’s openness and techno-publicity to spread ideology, organize, and maintain visibility. 
Analyzing interfaces, website infrastructure and user-generated content, this book reframes online extremism as a product of capitalism. 

Inside the book:

•	How algorithms, memes, and terms of service amplify online hate

•	The decline and rebirth of the alt-right

•	The political economy of platform capitalism

•	Abolitionism and internet politics

•	What the right talks about online when they think no one’s watching
